Who We Are

CropBay is a bridge between India's hardworking farmers and conscious consumers. Founded in 2023, we're on a mission to revolutionize the way fresh produce reaches your home by eliminating unnecessary middlemen and reducing food waste.

Our platform connects you directly with local farmers, ensuring you get the freshest products while farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work. Every purchase you make supports sustainable farming practices and helps preserve traditional agricultural knowledge.

Our Mission

To empower local farmers and provide consumers with fresh, affordable, and traceable farm products.

Our Vision

A future where every Indian household has access to fresh, local produce while farmers thrive economically.
